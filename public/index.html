NATURE. SCIENCE. SUSTAINABILITY.

Valorising the hidden potential of viticulture.

EcoStemTech is a scientific project (future UTAD spin-off) dedicated to the circular economy. We transform grape stems — a byproduct of the wine industry — into premium ingredients through patented green technology.

Our mission is to provide the industry with natural, effective, and sustainable alternatives that reduce environmental impact while enhancing product quality.

"Transforming winery by-products into high-value ingredients through science and innovation — delivering tailored solutions for a sustainable future."

🌱 Sustainability

We solve the waste problem by upcycling 2 million tonnes of EU winery residues into value.

🔬 Innovation

Our patented toasting and extraction methods are the result of years of academic research.

🍷 Quality

Our products are designed to meet the highest standards of the wine aging industry.

✦ Purity

Certified bioactive compounds for clean beauty and high-performance cosmetics.
