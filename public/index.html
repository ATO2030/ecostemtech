"Transforming the unseen value of winery by-products"

EcoStemTech is a university spin-off of the Universidade de Trás-os-Montes e Alto Douro (UTAD) and CITAB, created to valorise grape stems — a winery by-product representing 25% of all viticultural residues in Europe — into high-value solutions for two growing industries.

Our patented toasting process (EP 3 835 401 A1, valid in 38 European countries) transforms grape stems into a natural oenological ingredient that enhances wine complexity. Our green extraction process converts the same stems into certified bioactive compounds for cosmetic formulations.

"To transform grape stems into high-value bioactive ingredients through science, innovation and sustainability — delivering tailored solutions for the cosmetic, nutraceutical and wine industries."

🌱 The Problem

2 million tonnes of winery residues annually in Europe. Grape stems — 25% of that total — have near-zero commercial value despite being rich in bioactive compounds.

🔬 Our Patented Process

A controlled toasting method (EP 3 835 401 A1) and a green extraction process that unlock the full potential of grape stems for two distinct markets.

🍷 Line 1 — Viticulture

Toasted grape stems as a natural, eco-friendly alternative to oak wood in wine aging. 60% more cost-effective than French oak barrels.

✦ Line 2 — Cosmetics

Standardised bioactive extracts (polyphenols, resveratrol, stilbenes, tannins) for clean beauty formulations. COSMOS certification target.
